Gameplay Tips

Batting tips

  • Use Push early if wickets are falling.
  • Use Stroke when confidence is building.
  • Use Loft after confidence improves or when the target requires big shots.
  • Swipe direction before playing a shot to aim toward gaps.

Bowling tips

  • Use Good Length when you want control.
  • Use Yorker at higher speed for wicket pressure.
  • Use Spin at lower speed for variation.
  • Build bowler confidence with dot balls.
