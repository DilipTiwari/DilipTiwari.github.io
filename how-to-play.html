How to Play Cricket Career

Cricket Career is designed as a quick browser cricket match with cinematic presentation. The match begins with team selection, a toss ceremony, player entry, fielding discussion, batting or bowling controls, and post-match highlights.

1. Select match setup

Choose your team, opponent, jersey colour and match length. The current prototype supports short matches so it works smoothly on GitHub Pages and mobile browsers.

2. Watch the toss

Select Heads or Tails. The camera moves to the pitch, the coin is tossed, and the umpire declares the result. If your captain wins, you can choose to bat or bowl first.

3. Batting controls

  • Push: lower risk, useful for singles.
  • Stroke: balanced option for timing and boundaries.
  • Loft: high reward but higher wicket risk.
  • Swipe direction: drag on the field to aim the shot.

4. Bowling controls

Choose Good Length, Yorker, Bouncer or Spin and set the speed using the delivery speed meter. Each bowler type has a different ideal speed and confidence impact.

5. Confidence and special abilities

Boundaries and tight bowling increase confidence. Once confidence reaches the unlock level, special abilities such as Power Loft or Perfect Yorker become available.

6. Highlights

The game stores wickets, fours, sixes and fielding moments in the highlights list so users can review the match story after the result.
