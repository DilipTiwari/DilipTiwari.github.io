Teams

The current prototype includes national-style teams with fictional rosters. This gives a cricket feel without copying official squads or player likenesses.

Available teams

  • India XI
  • Australia XI
  • England XI
  • South Africa XI
  • New Zealand XI
  • Pakistan XI
  • Sri Lanka XI
  • Bangladesh XI

Why fictional players?

Using fictional names, original ratings and original animations helps keep the project independent and safer for advertising review. Licensed real-player assets can be added only when proper rights are available.

Cricket Career

A mobile-friendly original cricket game prototype with cinematic toss, field camera, batting, bowling and match highlights.

This is an independent fan-style cricket game concept. It is not affiliated with any cricket board, league, player, broadcaster or sponsor.
