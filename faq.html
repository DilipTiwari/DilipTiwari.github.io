Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cricket Career free?

Yes, the prototype is free to play in a browser.

Does the game use real players?

No. The current version uses original fictional player names and ratings. This is safer for publishing because it avoids copying player likenesses, official rosters or protected branding.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. The layout is responsive and the canvas supports touch swipes.

Why are there content pages?

The pages explain the game, rules and controls so visitors get helpful information and reviewers can understand the purpose of the site.

Does the game collect personal data?

The static prototype does not require login and does not store personal details on a server.

Cricket Career

A mobile-friendly original cricket game prototype with cinematic toss, field camera, batting, bowling and match highlights.

This is an independent fan-style cricket game concept. It is not affiliated with any cricket board, league, player, broadcaster or sponsor.
