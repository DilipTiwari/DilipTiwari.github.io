Privacy Policy

Cricket Career is a static browser game. The current version does not require account creation and does not ask users to submit personal information.

Information we collect

The game itself runs in the browser and does not send gameplay data to a private server. If analytics or advertising tools are added later, those services may collect information according to their own policies.

Cookies and advertising

This website may use Google AdSense or similar advertising services after approval. Advertising providers may use cookies or device identifiers to show, limit or measure ads.

Third-party links

The site may link to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

Children

The game is intended as general entertainment. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, update this page with your public contact email before publishing.
