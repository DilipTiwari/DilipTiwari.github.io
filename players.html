Player Ratings Guide

Every player in this prototype has an original fictional name, a role, a batting rating and a bowling rating. This keeps the game independent and avoids copying protected player likenesses.

Batting rating

A higher batting rating increases the chance of clean contact and reduces wicket risk.

Bowling rating

A higher bowling rating improves accuracy and wicket-taking chance.

Roles

Roles such as opener, anchor, finisher, fast bowler, spinner and all-rounder create different gameplay expectations.

Cricket Career

A mobile-friendly original cricket game prototype with cinematic toss, field camera, batting, bowling and match highlights.

This is an independent fan-style cricket game concept. It is not affiliated with any cricket board, league, player, broadcaster or sponsor.
