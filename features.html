Features

Cricket Career focuses on presentation and match feeling, not only button tapping.

Cinematic scenes

The game includes team selection, toss camera, coin toss animation, umpire decision, batter walk-in, fielding huddle, live broadcast camera, wicket entry and post-match result scene.

Umpire actions

The umpire can raise both arms for a six, signal a wicket, stand behind the bowler and appear during review-style moments.

Batting model

Batters have roles and ratings. Shot selection changes risk and reward. Swipe direction decides where the ball is aimed.

Bowling model

Bowlers have skill ratings. Speed, delivery type and confidence decide whether the ball is accurate, loose, wicket-taking or easy to score.

Fielding model

Fielders are shown around the ground and can create dot balls, super saves and catching moments.

Highlights model

Important events are saved so the player can view the match story after the game.

Cricket Career

A mobile-friendly original cricket game prototype with cinematic toss, field camera, batting, bowling and match highlights.

This is an independent fan-style cricket game concept. It is not affiliated with any cricket board, league, player, broadcaster or sponsor.
