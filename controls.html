Controls Guide

Touch and mouse aiming

Drag or swipe over the field to select the batting direction. The commentary confirms the direction after a valid swipe.

Shot buttons

Push is safe, Stroke is balanced, and Loft is aggressive. Choosing the right shot matters because each option changes the scoring and wicket chance.

Bowling buttons

Good Length is balanced, Yorker is attacking, Bouncer is aggressive and Spin uses lower speed. The speed slider decides whether the delivery is close to the ideal ball.

Special button

When confidence is high enough, the special button triggers a powerful batting or bowling ability.

Cricket Career

A mobile-friendly original cricket game prototype with cinematic toss, field camera, batting, bowling and match highlights.

This is an independent fan-style cricket game concept. It is not affiliated with any cricket board, league, player, broadcaster or sponsor.
