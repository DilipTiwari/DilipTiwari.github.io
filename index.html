Original web cricket game

Cinematic toss, live field camera, batting, bowling and highlights.

Cricket Career is a lightweight browser cricket game built for mobile and desktop. Select a national-style XI, choose your jersey colour, watch the toss ceremony, enter the field, play shots by style and direction, bowl with speed control, and review key match highlights after the final ball.

What is inside

Built as a proper site, not a blank game screen

The website includes original gameplay explanations, rules, feature pages, team details, privacy information and clear navigation so users and reviewers can understand the purpose of every page.

Toss ceremony

Captains and umpire appear on the pitch, the coin is tossed, the camera follows it, and the result is declared before the innings starts.

Batting and bowling

Choose Push, Stroke or Loft while batting, swipe to aim, or bowl with length, pace and confidence controls.

Match highlights

Fours, sixes, wickets, catches and super saves are saved in a post-match highlight list.
